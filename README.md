# BLACKOUT — 2026 FIFA World Cup, no spoilers

Catch up on the 2026 World Cup on your own schedule, without anyone spoiling the result.

- **No spoilers by default.** Scores and the bracket stay blacked out until you choose to reveal them, day by day.
- **Highlights in one place.** Official match highlights, organised by match, shown for your region.
- **Make your picks.** Fill in your own bracket prediction and save it as an image.
- **Nothing to install, no account, no API key.** Just open the site.

It tracks the real men's tournament (11 June – 19 July 2026). Match results come from the public‑domain [openfootball](https://github.com/openfootball/worldcup.json) dataset.

This is a non‑commercial fan project and is not affiliated with FIFA or any broadcaster. Broadcaster logos are used only to identify the source of each highlight.
